Mental Health Support in India

Kiran Mental Health Helpline

  • Helpline Number: 1800-599-0019
  • Availability: 24/7
  • Languages: Multiple languages available
  • Description: The Kiran Helpline is a national mental health support line launched by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment to assist individuals facing mental health challenges, including anxiety, depression, stress, and more severe mental health conditions. When calling this helpline, you will be connected with trained mental health professionals who offer counseling, emotional support, and guidance.

NIMHANS (National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences)

  • Location: Bangalore, Karnataka, India
  • Website: NIMHANS Official Website
  • Services: 24/7 helplines and online counseling services for mental health issues. Provides comprehensive support and information related to mental health disorders, substance abuse, rehabilitation, and crisis intervention.
  • Additional Information: NIMHANS is a leading mental health research institute in India and offers public access to resources on mental well-being, treatment options, and emergency care information.

iCALL - Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS)

  • Helpline Number: 9152987821
  • Availability: 8 AM to 10 PM (Monday to Saturday)
  • Description: iCALL is a free and confidential mental health helpline run by Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS). It provides assistance to anyone in emotional or psychological distress, offering services over phone, email, and chat. Trained counselors address a wide range of issues including stress, depression, relationship issues, and career concerns.

Samaritans Mumbai

  • Helpline Numbers: 9152987821, 9152987822, 9152987823
  • Availability: 3 PM to 9 PM, all days
  • Description: Samaritans Mumbai is a branch of Samaritans International and provides free, confidential emotional support to those feeling lonely, distressed, or suicidal. This service is particularly valuable for individuals needing someone to talk to but feeling uncomfortable seeking in-person help.
